# Week 1-2: Introduction to Physical AI

Is chapter mein hum Physical AI aur Humanoid Robotics ki duniya mein daakhil honge. Hum samjhenge ke kaise AI sirf digital spaces tak mehdood nahi raha, balkay ab physical world mein bhi kaam kar raha hai.

## 1. Foundations of Physical AI and Embodied Intelligence

*   **Digital AI vs. Physical AI:** Digital AI sirf software aur data par focus karta hai, jabke Physical AI robots ko physical world mein operate karne aur laws of physics ko samajhne mein madad karta hai.
*   **Embodied Intelligence:** Iska matlab hai AI ka aise physical body mein hona jo real world mein interact kar sake. Humanoid robots iski sab se behtareen misaal hain.
*   **Insani duniya mein Humanoid Robots ki ehmiyat:** Kyun Humanoid robots zaroori hain? Kyunki woh humari tarah physical form rakhte hain aur insani environments mein interact karke data se seekh sakte hain.

## 2. From Digital AI to Robots That Understand Physical Laws

*   AI models jo pehle sirf digital platforms par chalte the, ab physical world mein aa rahe hain.
*   Robots ko chalne, uthne, cheezein pakadne aur mahol ko samajhne ke liye physics ke qawaid ko samajhna zaroori hai.
*   **Sim-to-Real Transfer:** Simulation (virtual world) mein train kiye gaye AI models ko real robots (physical world) par implement karna.

## 3. Overview of Humanoid Robotics Landscape

*   **Tareekh aur maujooda haalat:** Humanoid robots kaise shuru hue aur aaj kahan tak pahunch gaye hain (e.g., Boston Dynamics, Unitree Robotics).
*   **Challenges:** Humanoid robots banane mein kya mushkilaat pesh aati hain (balance, movement, power, cost).
*   **Applications:** Humanoid robots kahan kahan use ho sakte hain (manufacturing, healthcare, service industry, exploration).

## 4. Sensor Systems: LIDAR, Cameras, IMUs, Force/Torque Sensors

Physical AI systems ko apne mahol ko samajhne ke liye mukhtalif sensors ki zaroorat hoti hai:

*   **LIDAR (Light Detection and Ranging):** Durr ki cheezon ka exact distance maloom karne ke liye light pulses use karta hai. Isse 3D maps banaye jaate hain.
*   **Cameras (Depth Cameras, RGB Cameras):**
    *   **RGB Cameras:** Rangin tasveerain aur videos lene ke liye. Insani aankhon ki tarah kaam karti hain.
    *   **Depth Cameras (e.g., Intel RealSense):** Har pixel ka distance maloom karti hain, jisse 3D perception behtar hoti hai (e.g., object detection, obstacle avoidance).
*   **IMUs (Inertial Measurement Units):** Robot ki orientation, angular velocity aur linear acceleration ko track karta hai (balance aur movement ke liye zaroori).
*   **Force/Torque Sensors:** Robot ke joron (joints) ya end-effectors (haath, panje) par lagne wali force ya torque ko measure karte hain. Isse robot cheezon ko sahi tareeqe se pakad sakta hai aur delicate tasks perform kar sakta hai.

Yeh chapter sirf ek introduction hai, aagey hum in topics ko mazeed tafseel se explore karenge.
